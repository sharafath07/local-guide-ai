# Kozhikode Local Guide Context

## City Identity
Kozhikode (Calicut) is a coastal city in Kerala, India.
It is known for Malabar cuisine, beaches, and a relaxed lifestyle.

## Food Culture
- Famous foods: Malabar biriyani, pathiri, kallummakkaya, beef fry
- Evening snacks are popular near beaches and town areas
- Sulaimani tea is commonly consumed after meals

## Local Language & Slang
Primary language: Malayalam

Common slang:
- "Scene illa" → No problem
- "Pwoli" → Awesome
- "Set aanu" → All good

## Daily Life & Habits
- Shops may close briefly during prayer times
- Evenings are busy near Kozhikode Beach
- Friday afternoons are usually less crowded

## Transport & Movement
- Common transport: buses, autos, bikes
- Traffic peak hours: 9–10 AM, 5–7 PM
- Short distances can take longer during evenings
