## Prompt 1: Food
**User:** Best evening snacks near Kozhikode Beach?

**AI Response:**
Popular evening snacks include kallummakkaya (mussels), pazham pori, and pathiri with beef fry. The beach area becomes active after 5:30 PM.

## Prompt 2: Slang
**User:** What does "scene illa" mean?

**AI Response:**
"Scene illa" is a Malayalam slang phrase meaning "no problem" or "it's fine". It is commonly used in casual conversations.

## Prompt 3: Traffic
**User:** How is traffic at 6 PM in Kozhikode?

**AI Response:**
Traffic is usually heavy between 5–7 PM due to office closing hours and beach visitors. Short distances may take longer during this time.
